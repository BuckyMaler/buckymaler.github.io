Apollo, is a minimal, thoughtfully designed single page website template, and serves as the perfect showcase for a professional photographer. The design was provided by the talented designer, Jakub Kowalczyk. Apollo, is grid framework independent, and fully responsive. Download available on GitHub.

The template was developed with usability at its forefront. The Jade offers simple readability, and the Sass is modular and well organized.

Finally, the template features two variety of carousel for displaying work.

Built With: Jade, Sass, jQuery
